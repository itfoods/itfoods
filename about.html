About Us



IT FOODS RICE TRADERS.

IT FOODS RICE TRADERS was established with a dynamic vision of becoming a leading exporter of Pakistan in the rice industry. Today IT FOODS RICE TRADERS is one of the leading exporters and providers of superior quality rice from Pakistan with over 20 years experience of domestic and international market of parboiled rice.
The team of I.T FOODS is dedicated and totally committed to maintain its quality, to asssure delivery in time and to build strong relations prestigious buyers. We are certified exporters of quality rice in Pakistan.
Being a certified member of Rice Exporters Association of Pakistan (REAP) and Bahawalpur Chamber Of Commerce And Industry we have established a true business model that has focused to global customer demand.
We have customer service department deedicated to provide complete information, services and support to each customer's query. Our team is dedicated and totally committed to our basic important principles which are mentioned below :

  • Strictly Follow Our Quality Control Standards.
  • Timely Delivery Schedules.
  • Competitive Offer.
  • Quality Guarantee (as per agreed rice specifications and approved samples).



OUR MISSION

Always comes upto the expectations of customers by creating the concept of Pure Quality Pakistani Rice at the most competitive prices to the International rice importing community and building long term relationships. It makes a concerted effort to give the best quality while using innovation and investment in leading-edge technology to work towards providing complete solutions The achievement of excellence of standards that we have setup for irreproachable quality of rice is made possible through constant vigilance of the entire process from procurement for marketing to offer a fair market and ethical practices Built on our ability to provide best and excellent Pakistani Rice with integrated Quality to meet the specific needs of the customers by understanding the nature of their business and requirement Excellence in Quality is our Aim.
